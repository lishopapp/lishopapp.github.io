Veladan is a future software products company. Its main focus is to create mobile applications for international distribution. Initially specializing in developing applications for iPhone and iPad.

This company is based on three pillars:

  • software
  • design
  • marketing

Combining this knowledge is essential to creating products for the end user.


Mission

Offering a set of Apps that are readily usable, enjoyable and useful.



Vision

Create a company whose products have a recognizable style, which will depend on the expertise of both the software development, design and marketing.

At present there are several applications for iPhone in the App Store.

LiShop iPhone App - Your shopping list

Zaragoza, Spain - November 22, 2012 - today is released LiShop 1.3.5, the easy of use shopping list app for your iPhone. The best app to register and never forget what to buy.

With this iPhone app you can take note of all those products you have to buy. At any time you register quickly that you should make those purchases.

Price & Availability

LiShop is free to purchase. But it has Ads. This Ads can be removed for 1,79 €.

Lishop is available for download worldwide. Any country with iTunes access can download and install LiShop.

About the project

One day I needed to find a shopping list app for my iPhone. I searched the App Store and found many apps, but the truth is that no one liked me. Some were very complex, some were very expensive, others were very ugly.

So I decided to create my own application. I made simple quick and easy to use: just as I want. I uploaded it to the App Store just to learn how it was done. What happened then is that many people downloaded the first version and received very good reviews. These comments encouraged me so good continue to create new versions.

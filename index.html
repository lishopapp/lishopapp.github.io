LiShop

To remember all you have to buy

Features

Ease of use

The easiest way to manage your shopping list. You can easily add new items. Arrange them by categories. Mark your prices and favorite stores.

You can quickly search for the item you wish to add: using the search engine indexing and column.

From any point of the app can edit the properties of any item.

Multilanguage

Supported languages: English, Spanish, Portuguese and Italian.

Share

You can send the list by email.

No Ads

The app has ads inside. But if you prefer to remove them, you can go to settings and elimiarlos at a cost.

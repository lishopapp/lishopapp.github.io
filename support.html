Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I remove the empty category?
This category can not be deleted. The app requires a default category.
Is there any restriction on number of items in the list?
There is no limit in the number of items you can create.
Can I back up article data?
In Configuration section, select backup option. Then follow the instructions showed in the screen.
Can I send the list to other people?
You can send the list by email. Tap the share button in the top-left in the "Shopping list" view.
